# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Planning Poker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Guest Users:**
- Display name (stored locally in your browser)
- User-generated content (room names, ticket descriptions, votes)

**Atlassian Authenticated Users:**
- Atlassian account ID
- Name
- Email address
- Profile picture (if available)
- Jira access tokens (for integration purposes)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

- **Session Data**: Room participation, voting activity, timestamps
- **Presence Information**: Last seen timestamps to show online status
- **Usage Data**: Room creation, ticket management, session activity

### 3. Third-Party Data

When you use Jira integration:
- Jira site information (cloud IDs, site names)
- Jira issue data (issue keys, summaries, descriptions)
- Story point updates synchronized to Jira

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Enable real-time collaboration in planning poker sessions
   - Display participant information and voting status
   - Manage rooms and tickets

2. **Jira Integration**
   - Search and link Jira issues
   - Synchronize story points to Jira
   - Display issue details within the app

3. **Improve User Experience**
   - Show online/offline status of participants
   - Maintain session state across page refreshes
   - Enable room creators to manage their rooms

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Locations

- **Firebase Firestore**: All session data, rooms, tickets, votes, and participant information
- **Browser Local Storage**: Guest user names and IDs (stored only on your device)
- **Session Storage**: Temporary authentication tokens

### Security Measures

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Firebase security rules restrict data access
- OAuth tokens are stored securely and never exposed to other users
- Atlassian access tokens are handled server-side only

### Data Retention

- **Active Sessions**: Data retained while room is active
- **Completed Sessions**: Data retained indefinitely unless manually deleted
- **User Accounts**: Guest user data stored locally; Atlassian user data retained per session
- **Deleted Rooms**: All associated data (tickets, votes, participants) permanently deleted

## Your Rights and Choices

### Guest Users

- **Data Control**: Your display name is stored only in your browser
- **Deletion**: Clear your browser's local storage to remove your guest user data
- **Participation**: You can leave rooms at any time

### Atlassian Users

- **Access**: View your profile information through your Atlassian account
- **Revoke Access**: Disconnect the app from your Atlassian account at any time
- **Data Deletion**: Contact room creators to remove your participation data

### Room Creators

- **Data Management**: Delete rooms and all associated data at any time
- **Participant Management**: Remove participants from rooms
- **Content Control**: Edit or delete tickets and votes you created

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
2. **Service Providers**: With Firebase and Atlassian to provide app functionality
3. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights
4. **Room Participants**: Your display name and votes are visible to other participants in the same room
